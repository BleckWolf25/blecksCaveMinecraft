## ❓Frequently Asked Questions

### ⚔️ Gameplay & Mechanics

**Q. I can't punch trees to get wood. How do I start?!**

**A.** Check your Questbook! You are a mortal, not a superhero. You must forage the ground for loose pebbles, flint, and twigs to craft your very first primitive hatchet. Punching blocks bare-handed will actively damage your limbs and hand structure.

**Q. The game is way too hard!**

**A.** If you are really struggling, you can try disabling or removing **Zombie Awareness** and/or **Enhanced AI**. This will make the game much easier, but keep in mind it might cause unexpected balance issues and/or make things *too* easy.

**Q. Why am I suddenly so slow/blind/losing hearts?**

**A.** You likely took structural damage to a specific limb. This effect is worsened if you are wearing heavy plate armor, which restricts vision and drains your stamina pools quickly. Leather armor, on the other hand, grants a flat movement speed increase.

**Q. How does multiplayer death actually work?**

**A.** If you play solo, death is permanent. On a multiplayer server, dropping to 0 HP puts you in a "downed" bleeding state. Friends can revive you without medicine, but as a penalty, you will permanently lose 1 Max Heart from your total health pool.

**Q. Where are the creepers? How do I get gunpowder?**

**A.** Creepers and Witches were completely banished from the game engine because they do not fit the medieval thematic landscape. If you need gunpowder, you will need to find alternative recipes to craft it manually.

**Q. Can I play this with a controller?**

**A.** The dynamic, directional combat *might* feel incredible on a controller! However, you will need to add a controller support mod yourself. Do so at your own risk, keeping the Technical Notes above in mind.

### ⚙️ Performance & Troubleshooting

**Q. I have a beefy PC / >=8GB of RAM allocated, but my game still lags or stutters.**

**A.** Try the following steps to improve performance:
1. Make sure your Render and Simulation Distances aren't set too high (quality presets).
2. Try disabling or removing the **Subtle Effects** mod.
3. Lower your overall graphics settings to "Fast".
4. If using shaders, lower their quality settings and ensure your GPU drivers are up to date.

**Q. I want to play with shaders. What is the most lightweight/compatible option?**

**A.** Currently, the best option for both performance and compatibility is **MakeUp-Ultra Fast**. It still looks decent with almost no impact on your performance, and it works great across Windows, macOS, and Linux.

**Q. Why is my game sometimes capped at 60 FPS?**

**A.** This MIGHT be caused by the **FPS Reducer** mod. The easiest fix is to `Alt+Tab` out of the game, and then physically *click* the Minecraft icon on your taskbar to tab back in. Your FPS should return to your normal cap.

**Q. I see ERRORS/WARNINGS in my launch console, is this normal?**

**A.** Yes! Most errors and warnings are completely normal background noise from Minecraft and Java.

### 📦 General & Modpack Info

**Q. How do I play multiplayer?**

**A.** Create/join a world -> ESC -> Open to LAN and there you go, a link in few seconds will appear, copy that and send to your friends and they just have to direct connect, simple! (E4MC not essential mod).

**Q. Can I download this modpack, modify it myself, and publish it?**

**A.** Yes! Just make sure to credit the original modpack with a link in your description.

**Q. Will you port Aetas Ferrea to Fabric, NeoForge, or newer versions like 1.21?**

**A.** Likely not. The highly specific AI, siege structures, and mechanics that make this pack work are fundamentally tied to 1.20.1 Forge. Porting it would mean waiting for the original creators to update their mods, and I would have to reconfigure the entire modpack from scratch. *(Fun fact: At the start of this project, I actually tried developing it on NeoForge 1.21.1!)*

**Q. Why can't I remove certain resource packs?**

**A.** These packs are locked by design to ensure a consistent experience for everyone, especially on multiplayer servers. While resource packs can easily be used to exploit or cheat (like using X-ray packs), keeping them mandatory helps ensure everyone plays fairly and stays on the same team! (even tho this is easily exploitable!!!)

**Q. I see unknown versions/authors on some mods, is this normal?**

**A.** Yes. I have custom-modified a lot of mods to include features and/or fixes they didn't originally have. Some authors are still listed if I was able to tag them, but others are missing because they don't have an account on this platform or the java file bugged out.

**Q. How can I report issues?**

**A.** Scroll down this page until you find the "Official GitHub Repository for Aetas Ferrea" link and click it, then head to the "Issues" tab to create a new ticket. Alternatively, just click the "Issues" button at the top of the Modrinth page.

**Q. How can I give you a donation or compensation?**

**A.** I don't accept any forms of donations! I'm perfectly fine, I made this modpack as a hobby and just doing it for fun.

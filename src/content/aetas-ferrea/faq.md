## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### ⚔️ Gameplay & Mechanics

**Q. I can't punch trees to get wood. How do I start?!**

**A.** Check your Questbook! You are a mortal, not a superhero. You must forage the ground for loose pebbles, flint, and twigs to craft your very first primitive hatchet.

**Q. The game is way too hard!**

**A.** If you are really struggling, you can try disabling or removing **Zombie Awareness** and/or **Enhanced AI**. This will make the game much easier, but keep in mind it might cause unexpected balance issues.

**Q. Why am I suddenly so slow/blind/losing hearts?**

**A.** You likely took damage to a specific limb. This effect is worsened if you are wearing full plate armor or carrying a heavy weapon in your inventory.

**Q. How does multiplayer death actually work?**

**A.** If you play solo, death is permanent. On a multiplayer server, dropping to 0 HP puts you in a "downed" bleeding state. Friends can revive you without medicine, but as a penalty, you will permanently lose 1 Max Heart.

**Q. Where are the creepers? How do I get gunpowder?**

**A.** Creepers were removed because they do not fit the theme of this modpack. If you need gunpowder, you will need to craft it instead.

### ⚙️ Performance & Troubleshooting

**Q. I have a beefy PC, but my game still lags or stutters.**

**A.** Try the following steps to improve performance:

1. Make sure your Render and Simulation Distances aren't set too high (quality presets).
2. Try disabling or removing the **Subtle Effects** mod.
3. Lower your overall graphics settings to "Fast".

**Q. I want to play with shaders. What is the most lightweight/compatible option?**

**A.** Currently, the best option for both performance and compatibility is **MakeUp-Ultra Fast**. It still looks decent with almost no impact on your performance.

## 💀 *"Memento Mori"* - Hardcore & Multiplayer Penalties
*Remember that you must die.* Face off against the world in strict Hardcore mode. If you die in single-player, the world ends.

**Multiplayer Support:** Play with friends, but death still carries a heavy toll. Teammates can revive you from a downed state, but the revived player will permanently lose 1 Max Heart from their total health pool. Choose who you save wisely.

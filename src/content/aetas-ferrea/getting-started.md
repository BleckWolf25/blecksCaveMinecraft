Your goal for Day 1 is to secure a **Class**, a **Knife**, and **Basic Shelter Materials** before nightfall.

## Step 1: Choose a Class & Gather Tools

- **Pick a Class:** Open your **Quest Book** right away to select your starting class.
- **Gather Flint:** Look for loose rocks on the ground.
  - *Standard loose rocks:* 5% flint drop chance.
  - *Gravel loose rocks:* 50% flint drop chance.
- **Gather Sticks:** Look for loose sticks on the ground (common near forests) or break leaf blocks until one drops.
- **Craft a Flint Knife:** Combine your flint and stick.

## Step 2: Resource Harvesting

- **Gather Straw:** Use your new Flint Knife to destroy grass and tall grass.
- *Note:* Tall grass has a significantly higher straw drop rate than normal grass.
- **Craft Rope:** Combine 2x Straw in your crafting grid.
- **Chop Wood:** Use the Flint Knife to chop down trees.
- *Warning:* Using a knife on logs inflicts **20%+ more durability damage** to the tool.

## Step 3: Establish Your Arsenal

Gather enough wood to craft the essential baseline items:

- Crafting Table
- Chest
- Wooden Axe
- Wooden Pickaxe

> ⚠️ **Weapon Warning:** Wood is soft and lightweight. While wooden weapons attack quickly, they deal low trauma damage and are completely ineffective against armored enemies. Attacking armor will quickly break your tool.

## Step 4: The Essentials Checklist

Before the sun sets, gather these materials for your tier-two upgrades:

| Material | Quantity | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Straw** | 12x | Crafting 3x Canvas (for a Bed) |
| **Wood Planks** | A few stacks | Building blocks and tools |
| **Stone / Mossy Cobble** | Varied | Upgrading to Stone Tools |
| **Coal** *(Optional)* | Varied | Crafting Torches |

Using these resources, immediately craft a **Bed**, upgrade to **Stone Tools**, and light up your area with **Torches**.

## Step 5: Final Preparation (Nightfall)

If you have time left over, gather the resources for a **Campfire** to survive the night:

- 1x Flint
- 2x Straw
- 3x Stick
- 3x Logs

**If you run out of time:** Build a quick mini-fort or dig a temporary hole underground.

> 🚨 **Critical Danger:** Do not feel entirely safe behind walls—the undead **can mine through blocks** if they are equipped with the correct tools!

If you managed to gather everything, your survival chance has highly increased, now you should be able to make it to Chapter 2!

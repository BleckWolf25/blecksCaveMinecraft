# 🛡️ Aetas Ferrea - Hardcore Medieval Survival

> _"Natura non contristatur."_  
> _(Nature does not care.)_

🧭 **Aetas Ferrea: The Iron Age**

_Sanguis et ferrum._
Blood and iron. You are a remnant of a fallen kingdom, thrust into an unforgiving wilderness where civilization has crumbled. The world is cold, brutal, and indifferent to your survival.

After a catastrophic collapse the lands are now ruled by advanced predators, starvation, and ancient mythical beasts that have awakened in the deep... Will you conquer the Iron Age, or become another forgotten skeleton in the mud?

_"Aetas Ferrea"_ Iron age is a modpack built entirely around realistic physical and hardcore survival. Get ready to experience a brutal, realistic medieval world.

Focusing on extreme mechanical friction while providing feature-rich combat and exploration you aren't just building a place to call home. You are going to be establishing a self-sustaining fortified kingdom to survive intelligent monster sieges. You decide your path: live as a nomad, or forge a fortress capable of withstanding the end of the world.

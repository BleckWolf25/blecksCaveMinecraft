## MCVT: What is it?

**MCVT is a modpack designed to enhance the vanilla Minecraft experience**, **focusing** on both **performance improvements and feature expansions**. If you're bored of playing the same old Minecraft, MCVT offers a refreshing take with mods/resource packs customizability, making to players who prefer performance optimizations, feature-rich gameplay, or both.

This modpack is highly modifiable and works seamlessly with vanilla servers, making it ideal as a client-side modpack. While it's primarily for client use, some mods also support server-side functionality.

---

## Why Choose MCVT?

* **Customizable:** Choose what you’re after for, pure performance boosts or/and new features.
* **Future support for Older Versions:** Play your favorite Minecraft versions with modern optimizations and features (within compatibility & version limits).

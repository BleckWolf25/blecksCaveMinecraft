# Aetas Ferrea - Spartan Integration

An official resource pack expansion for the Aetas Ferrea modpack that completely re-imagines some Spartan Weaponry items. It replaces the default flat models with high-fidelity, photorealistic textures designed to match a medieval aesthetic. Fully equipped with PBR (LabPBR standard) normal and specular maps, weapons interact dynamically with shader lighting, casting shadows on sweeps and reflecting metallic highlights.

## Features

- **Detailed Weapon Overrides:** Completely visual overhauls for Scythes, Glaives, Flanged Maces, Battleaxes, Longswords, Greatswords, Daggers, and more.
- **Realistic Weapon Profiles:** Symmetrical double-edged battleaxe blades, historically accurate gothic-style flanged maces with concave spines, and sweeping curved scythes.
- **3D Shaded Depth:** Embedded normal maps (\_n) and specular maps (\_s) bring out metallic glint, wood grain details, and leather grips under PBR-enabled shaders.

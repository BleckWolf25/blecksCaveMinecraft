# Aetas Ferrea - Extras Integration

Polishes and replaces select items from utility and decorative mods in the Aetas Ferrea modpack. It overrides textures for FTB Quests, Serene Seasons, and Immersive Paintings with high-fidelity, medieval-inspired.

## Features

- **FTB Quests:** Re-imagines the Quest Book with a highly polished golden embossed star on a leather cover, and overhauls the Loot Crate Opener block to look like a heavy medieval spruce and brass crank station.
- **Serene Seasons:** Overhauls the pocket calendar into a textured parchment flip-book bound in leather with brass loops. Features unique hand-drawn medieval seasonal illustrations for all 12 sub-seasons (roses for Spring, suns for Summer, maple leaves for Autumn, snowflakes for Winter, rainclouds for Wet, and cracked suns for Dry).
- **Immersive Paintings:** Replaces standard paintings and graffiti items with rustic parchment scrolls bound in leather cords and clay paint jars containing paintbrushes. Includes neon-glowing slime ink variants for glow paintings and glow graffiti.

---

**Dependencies:** Immersive Paintings, Serene Seasons, FTB Quests.

# Aetas Ferrea - Farmer's Delight Integration

Complete visual integration for Farmer's Delight within the Aetas Ferrea modpack. It takes culinary tools, storage units, and farming ropes and upgrades them to photorealistic 32x resolution with full LabPBR shader compatibility.

## Features

- **Culinary Knives (Flint, Iron, Gold, Diamond):** Overhauled from thin sticks to professional kitchen knives featuring a thick wood/leather grip, a solid steel bolster guard, a flat straight spine, and a curved cutting edge.
- **Seasoned Cast-Iron Cooking Pot:** The cooking pot is designed as dark, textured seasoned cast-iron. The top texture renders a boiling vegetable stew with chunks of carrots and herbs under shiny liquid specularity.
- **Cabinets & Woven Baskets:** Overhauls all 11 wood cabinet styles with panel-recessed closed doors and open states showing shelves stacked with food rolls and jars. Baskets are styled as realistically woven wood and bamboo.
- **Twisted Ropes & Safety Nets:** Ropes are designed with wavy, coiled outer borders and diagonal fiber normals that create a realistic 3D rope look. Fences and gates wrap these ropes tightly around oak posts without missing pixels.

# Aetas Ferrea - MineColonies Integration

An aesthetic pack for the Aetas Ferrea modpack that updates Minecolonies and Structurize tool assets. It replaces the cartoonish default items with realistic, medieval counterparts that match Patrix 32x wood, steel, brass, and crystal textures.

## Features

- **Work Hammers:** Replaces the bulky superhero-style hammers with realistic, historically-grounded stone/metal mason sledges and framing hammers.
- **Scepters (Gold, Steel, Tag, Lumberjack, Guard, Beekeeper, Permission):** Fully detailed scepters featuring leather-wrapped handles topped with medieval finials (brass key collars, gold crowns, raw wood carvings, glowing emeralds, and yellow-striped bee domes).
- **Tools:** Overhauls the Caliper and Shape Tool (featuring a glowing cyan crystal focus) to look like authentic medieval measuring and drafting instruments.
- **Substitution Blocks:** Replaces the grid frames with heavy steel-riveted borders holding wood planks, rippling water planes, or hollow centers.

---

**Dependencies:** MineColonies, Structurize.
